# Grandma's gone missing
Grandma’s gone missing is a short exploration game about Little Red going to visit her grandma, but once arriving to the cabin, grandma is nowhere to be found. Search the cabin for clues to find out what might’ve happened.

Controls: Moving - WASD + mouse; Interacting - E

Music sourced from Pixabay: Acoustic Guitar & Flute Fairytale Music by Denis Pavlov Music; Jazz Background Music by Tunetank; Forest Daytime bu CapaholiczSFX
